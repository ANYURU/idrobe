# Welcome to iDrobe

This is the initial release of iDrobe - your smart wardrobe assistant.

## Features

- **Wardrobe Management**: Organize all your clothing items
- **AI Recommendations**: Get personalized outfit suggestions
- **Analytics**: Track your wardrobe usage and trends
- **Sustainability**: Monitor your fashion footprint

## Getting Started

Start by adding your first clothing items to your wardrobe!
